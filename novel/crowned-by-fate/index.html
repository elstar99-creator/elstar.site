Crowned by Fate

By Elstar

Blurb

She was forced to marry the cold and distant King — a man who rejected her heart before the vows were even spoken. In a kingdom where love is weakness and loyalty is a weapon, she is treated as nothing but a pawn in a game of power.

But fate has other plans.

As secrets unfold and alliances shift, the girl once ignored rises from silence. Behind the crown lies a fire no one expected — not even the King who cast her aside.

Now he wants what he once threw away... But will she still choose him when she finally learns the truth?

A royal romance of betrayal, survival, and a love written in the stars.
